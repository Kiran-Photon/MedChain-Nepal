About MedChain

Protecting healthcare through blockchain transparency and immutable supply chain verification.

🎯 Our Mission

MedChain is dedicated to combating counterfeit medicines by leveraging Solana blockchain technology. We provide transparent, immutable records of every medicine's journey from manufacturer to patient.

⛓️ How It Works

1. Registration

Every medicine is registered on Solana blockchain with cryptographic signatures.

2. Supply Chain Tracking

Each transfer (manufacturer → distributor → pharmacy) is recorded on-chain.

3. Verification

Customers verify medicine authenticity using QR codes and Solana transactions.

🌐 Technology Stack

Blockchain: Solana Devnet
Storage: IPFS
Smart Contracts: Solana Programs
Verification: QR Codes
